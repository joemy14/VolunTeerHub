Bantay Hayop Davao

Type: On-Site Position

Description

Program Overview: Bantay Hayop Davao is a Sec Registered NGO that promotes Animal Welfare, Responsible pet ownership, and an Animal Friendly Davao City.

Aside from rescuing, rehabilitation and adoption of dogs, cats, and sometimes snakes, BHD also files cases against animal cruelty, empowers people to stand against dog meat trading and eating, holds symposiums and education regarding animal welfare, and more.
